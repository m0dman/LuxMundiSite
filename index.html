What You'll Get
We live in a C2 with HS and C3 statics giving easy access to k-space, j-space explo and lower class ratting.
As Lux Collective is based in a wormhole we have a constantly changing chain to explore, loot and generally enjoy. Using PathFinder you can benefit from the alliance's scanning to see all the chain has to offer without having to scan it all yourself. This means there is always access to any kind of activity New Eden has to offer. With our infrastructure we offer very low taxes and indexes for industry, with our veteran FCs we offer boosted mining/huffing fleets and with our blood-thirsty PVPers we offer any kind of ship exploding activities you might wish for.
